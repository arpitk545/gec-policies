Data Protection

Codrex — AI-Powered College Learning Platform

Last updated: July 2026

Codrex is committed to protecting user data and ensuring responsible, transparent data handling across our website, mobile app, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1Data Storage

User data is stored securely using industry-standard safeguards and is accessed only when necessary to operate, maintain, or improve the Service.

2Data Sharing

We do not sell or misuse user data. Data may be shared with third parties only where necessary to operate the Service (such as hosting or analytics providers) or where required by law.

3Data Minimization

We collect only the information reasonably needed to provide and improve Codrex, and avoid retaining data beyond what is necessary for that purpose.

4User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about them.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of their personal data, subject to applicable legal requirements.

5Compliance

We follow basic data protection principles — fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, and security — in how we collect, store, and process user data.
