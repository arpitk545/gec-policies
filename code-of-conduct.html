The AIML Club of GEC Khagaria is committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment.

Expected Behavior

  • Treat everyone with respect and professionalism
  • Encourage learning, collaboration, and healthy discussion
  • Follow instructions during workshops, events, and meetings

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
  • Disruptive behavior during events or discussions
  • Misuse of club platforms or resources

Reporting Issues

Any violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to the club coordinators or faculty advisors.

Enforcement

The club reserves the right to take appropriate action, including warnings or removal from activities, in case of violations.
